Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blank Token?

+

Blank Token (BNK) is a community-driven cryptocurrency on the BNB Chain that starts with zero predefined utility. It's a blank canvas where the community decides the token's purpose and direction through decentralized governance.

How can I buy Blank Token?

+

You can buy BNK on PancakeSwap by connecting your wallet and swapping BNB for BNK. Make sure you're on the BNB Chain network in your wallet before making the swap.

How does governance work?

+

BNK holders can create and vote on proposals that determine the future of the project. Each BNK token represents one vote. Proposals can include anything from token utility changes to treasury allocation.

What's the total supply?

+

The total supply of BNK is 25,000,000 tokens. This is a fixed supply with no minting function, making BNK a deflationary asset.
